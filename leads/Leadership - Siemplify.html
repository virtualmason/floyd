Leadership

Amos Stern
Co-Founder & CEO
Amos is the Chief Executive Officer at Siemplify. He brings a unique technical and business background that includes leadership of the Cyber Security department within the IDF Intelligence Corps as well as directing sales and business development for Elbit’s Cyber & Intelligence Division.
Alon Cohen
Co-Founder and CTO
With rich experience in Israel’s intelligence agency and Elbit Cyber & Intelligence division, Alon brings extensive technological and development-management background. He is a renowned expert in intelligence analysis systems, graph-analysis and defensive cyber solutions development.
Garry Fatakhov
Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer
Garry is a seasoned entrepreneur with rich and diverse experience spanning both business and technology, His experience ranges from algorithmic research in algorithmic trading, cyber security and intelligence, to driving revenue and penetrating new markets.
Danielle Hefetz-Cohen
Chief Financial Officer
Danielle heads up the finance department, bringing over 20 years of Senior Financial Management in corporate finance, strategic planning, M&A, modeling and analysis. Previously, Danielle has served as CFO at PlugIN Global and Kalisaya Ltd, VP Business Operations at Comverse Technology (CMVT acquired by Siris Capital)
Dennis Shaya
GM & Head of U.S. Operations
Dennis has a broad background in multiple operating, management and investment roles. He began his career in management consulting with Deloitte consulting, was part of the founding team of an early stage digital marketplace in the dot-com boom, and served in multiple corporate incubators. Most recently he was a partner with the NYC based venture firm Abundance Partners.
Nimmy Reichenberg
Chief Marketing Officer
Nimmy oversees all marketing activities including strategy, demand generation, communications and branding. An industry security veteran, Nimmy brings nearly 20 years of marketing leadership experience in high-growth companies. Nimmy previously led marketing for eSentire, AlgoSec and NextNine.
Meny Har
VP, Products
Meny guides Siemplify's product vision and roadmap. Prior to Siemplify, he led the SOC Methodology, integrations and training center for ST InfoSecurity. He was also a Cyber Solutions Architect for Elbit Systems LTD, and served as a cyber security department head for the IDF’s central command.
Moran Borenstein
VP, Engineering
Moran leads the Engineering group, bringing over 16 years of experience in software development. Before joining Siemplify, Moran was the head of the R&D department in the Israeli Intelligence Corps Technological Unit (Cyber Department), managing teams that worked on various cybersecurity initiatives.
Stefan Schmid
VP, Sales, EMEA
Stefan drives international expansion in Europe, Middle East and Africa. He brings over 15 years of leadership experience in building world wide sales teams. Prior to joining Siemplify, Stefan lead sales teams with HPE and TippingPoint
Wayne Goeckeritz
VP, Global Channels
Wayne is responsible for the recruitment, growth, and well being of our Global Channels and Alliance Partners. His entire career has been spent in the IT marketplace beginning with his first channel roles at both Cisco & Juniper. He has held Channel leadership roles at several startups including Crossbeam, Netwitness, Prolexic, and Demisto. Most recently, having left Palo Alto Networks managing their Global Channel for the Cortex product line.
Michael Hardwicke Brown
VP, Demand Generation
Mike oversees all growth marketing, including digital, demand generation, field, operations and analytics. With over 15 years' B2B SaaS & software marketing experience and a strong technical background, he also brings knowledge and experience to systems, processes and the overall tech stack.
Liz Glaichman
VP People Operations
Liz leads all people operations at Siemplify, ensuring we recruit, develop and retain the best talent while maintaining and evolving our unique corporate culture.

Bill Wiberg
