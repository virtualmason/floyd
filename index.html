About Me

I'll never forget one of my first full time inside sales jobs. I was selling Dish Network cable around the year 1999. One day the owner of the company comes in and says "whoever doesn't have the book Mastering The Art Of Sales By Tom Hopkins on your desk by Monday is fired"!

Since then, I read the books, took the classes, practiced, drilled and rehearsed the art of sales, so I can stay in the top 5% of sales people in the country. That thirst of knowledge led me to self learn full-stack web application development, marketing and psychology in order not to become outdated in my sales approach.

The point is, I know how to think and solve sales problems and pass that knowledge to others. That knowledge has served me well, setting up the sales processes and systems for startups, being the first productive sales hire and sales strategist for tech startups like Siemplfy, making quota at Constant Contact and using inbound and outbound selling strategies in my own ventures over the years. I have closed 6 figure deals and ready to remove friction from your sales system using modern techniques!
